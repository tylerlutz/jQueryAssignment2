Question Answer
How do I play the jQuery TicTac Toe game? First, you will need to enter the players names into the textboxes. If you attempt to enter just one players name and error will be displayed into the message box. After the names are entered, player 1 may select a box and then player 2 until the game is over.
How do I know who wins? The message box displays everything you need to know including the current player, and any errors that may occur. If the game has been won, the message box will display the player who has won.
What if there is an error? If an error occurs a message will be presented to you in the message box. If this problem still occurs, then let us know using the Contact page.
How do I register? To register, click on the Registration Form in the navigation bar. Fill out the information and click on submit.
What information is needed to register? We will need your first name, last name, email address, and then you create a password to use with the site.
Do I have to give my email address? Yes, you email address is going to used as your username for the site. Also we will need your email to send you password resets in case you forget your password.
Will my email be used for spam? Absolutely not. We only use your email address as your username and to send you password reset links.
If I have a question who can I contact? We are always looking into ways to improve our site. If you have any questions or concerns, send it to use trough the Contact Form.
How long does it take you to get back to me? It takes us about 2-3 business days to get back to you. If it is longer then that, please send us another message in case we did not get it.
How was this game created? This game was created using simple HTML, CSS, Javascript, and jQuery. Code for this game can be found on Github.
What if I forget my password? Forget your password? Go to the login page and click on the password reset button.
What is the process of reseting my password? Click on the password reset button on the login page and then enter your email address. Click on the reset link in the email you received, and then enter in your new password.
I did not get a password reset link. If you did not get a password reset link, check you email's spam box.
Will there be a leaderboard? Eventually we will add a leaderboard. But until then, enjoy our great game!
What features will be added in the future? A leaderboard will be coming very soon, eventually we would like to have online competitions.
I have a feature request. If you have a feature request let us know! Use the Contact Form to send us a message.
Any job openings? We are always looking for great people to be added to our team. Let us know you are interested using the Contact Form and we can get back to you.
Are there other ways to contact you? Currently there is only one way to contact us, and that is through the contact form. Other forms of contact could potentially be offered in the future.
How was this FAQ page made? This page was created using the jQuery Data Tables plugin.
How is my information validated? All information enter into our forms are validated using the jQuery validation plugin.
